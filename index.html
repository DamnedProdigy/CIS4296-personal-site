Projects

I've worked on some neat things.
Video Game Review (VGR) Website

In my CIS 3342 class, I develped a video game review website with a partner. This website has the ability of having three different account types (Publisher, Reviewer, & Consumer). A publisher is able to add a game, a reviewer is able to give their review on the game, while also displaying a badge next to their username letting people know they're a legitimate reviewer (also shows w/ a publisher & consumer). A regular consumer can post a comment underneath a game as well, letting customers & others know their opinion on a game. This website also has the ability to show an average via a star rating system. It also displays the price for the game, which can be edited by the publisher if need be.
